Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, place an order, or communicate with us. This includes your name, email address, phone number, physical address (for pickup and delivery), and payment information.

We also automatically collect location data (if you grant permission) to track deliveries and pickups in real-time.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our laundry and dry cleaning services.
  • Process your transactions and send you related information.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Communicate with you about products, services, offers, and events.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information with our delivery partners (drivers) only to the extent necessary to fulfill your pickup and delivery requests. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage your account settings within our mobile application or use our Account Deletion Portal.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@rideallaundry.com.
